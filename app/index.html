Charity Foundation

I was co-owner of the Charity Foundation "Let's see the Victory".

The mission of our Charitable Foundation is to help people who lost their sight during the war. Unfortunately, eye transplantation is impossible, so people's lives change radically after such an injury.

The Foundation is engaged in the search for necessary rehabilitation programs and modern vision restoration technologies in Ukraine and abroad, as well as in implementing existing methods and technologies for vision restoration and rehabilitation of blind people in Ukraine.
